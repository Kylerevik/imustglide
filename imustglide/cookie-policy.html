Understanding how we use cookies on our website

Last Modified: December 20, 2024

Website: https://imustglide.gg

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy.

🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us enhance your experience, analyze our traffic, and serve personalized content and advertisements.

🎯 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our website and improve its performance, we use cookies from Google Analytics and Hotjar. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymous data, such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and general geographic location (not exact).
  • Advertising: We use cookies from Google Ads and X Pixel to display targeted advertisements that may be of interest to you. These cookies help us understand the effectiveness of our ads and ensure that we're reaching our audience effectively.

📋 Types of Cookies We Use

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These are essential for the functioning of our website and cannot be disabled in our systems.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources to improve website performance. They help us understand which pages are most and least popular and how visitors move around the site.

3. Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to personalize ads shown to you based on your interests and previous interactions with our website.
